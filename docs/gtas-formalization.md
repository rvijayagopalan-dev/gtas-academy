If your goal is to evolve GTAS from an interesting synthesis into a serious scientific, engineering, and product discipline, the next logical step is **formalization**.

Right now, GTAS is primarily a **conceptual meta-framework**. To become a discipline comparable to Systems Theory, Control Theory, Information Theory, or Computer Science, it needs a rigorous foundation.
