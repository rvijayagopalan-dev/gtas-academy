## Viability Function

V=AC-EC

Where:

* AC = Adaptive Capacity
* EC = Environmental Change

Interpretation:

```text
V > 0 → Sustainable
V = 0 → Critical
V < 0 → Collapse Risk
```

# Grand Vision Statement

> GTAS seeks to establish a universal science of adaptation capable of explaining how systems learn, evolve, coordinate, and persist across scales ranging from intelligent agents to civilizations and planetary ecosystems.
>
> Its engineering realization, UEEOS, provides the operating framework through which adaptive intelligence, governance, digital twins, knowledge systems, and collective decision-making can be integrated into sustainable adaptive enterprises, nations, and civilizations.
>
> The ultimate objective is to enable humanity to build systems that are not merely intelligent, but resilient, self-improving, ethically governed, and capable of thriving across centuries of change.
